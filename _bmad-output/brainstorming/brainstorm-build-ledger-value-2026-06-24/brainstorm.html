Build Ledger?

The mirror

Personal insight

An /insights-style self-knowledge tool: am I actually growing as an engineer, or just busy? The engineering diary never written by hand.

01Technique13 ideas · wave 1

Job to Be Done

What is this thing hired to do — outward, and inward?

External jobs · the window

01Make a skeptical hiring manager believe my AI-assisted work is real engineering in under 60 seconds — the GL job.
02Give me ONE link that ends the "did you actually build that?" conversation.
03Apply to agentic roles without rewriting a portfolio each time — one living artifact.
04Prove seniority and scope (systems, not tickets) without name-dropping employers or breaking NDAs.
05Convert a cold DM into a reply by being un-ignorable.

Personal jobs · the mirror

06Tell me whether I'm actually growing as an engineer — or just busy.
07Show which systems are alive vs quietly dead, so I know where my real leverage is.
08Catch my own drift — am I sliding from building systems to babysitting agents?
09Give me evidence to keep shipping on impostor-weeks: evidence over feelings.
10Help me decide what to build next by showing where I'm thin — no tests? one language? no docs?
11Settle whether AI made me faster, or just noisier.
12Be the engineering diary I never have to write.
02Technique12 ideas · wave 1

How Might We

Reframe each desire as an open question — a problem stated so it begs to be solved.

HMW · prove judgment

Surface "decisions made" — ADRs, things deliberately NOT built, reverts with their reasoning.

HMW · count private work

Redacted silhouettes: shape and complexity shown, identity hidden. Private fintech platform · 84k LOC · 410 tests.

HMW · show AI fluency as craft

Auto-extract a "how I work with agents" playbook from real CLAUDE.md / AGENTS.md files.

HMW · make it feel alive

A "this week in my engineering" auto-digest — a changelog of the ledger itself.

HMW · make a recruiter get it

One-line plain-English verdict per system — e.g. "a live mapping platform for retail real estate."

HMW · turn a number into a story

Click any metric → the commits, files and evidence behind it. Drill-to-proof.

HMW · motivate without vanity

Frame streaks as "deep work seasons," not gamified counters.

HMW · de-risk the "AI wrote it" jab

Show the human-in-the-loop — review comments, eval failures caught, agent mistakes you fixed.

HMW · make it comparable

"You vs you, 12 months ago" instead of you vs other people.

HMW · be credible at a glance

Open the actual repo or file from the page — evidence one click from source.

HMW · capture non-code work

Treat infra, pipelines, prompt systems, evals and dashboards as first-class builds.

HMW · make it worth revisiting

Auto-draft a monthly engineering retro from the data.

Synthesis · the session resolves

The mirror is the window.

self-knowledge ≡ external proof · one artifact, two faces

Build the self-insight tool first. Self-directed honesty turns out to be the unfakeable external signal — it dissolves the old trap of "a resume pretending not to be one." A skeptic can tell the difference between a sales pitch and a person genuinely auditing themselves; the second is the only one that converts.

Connection 01 · the reframe

Mirror = Window

The biggest insight of the session: the personal self-knowledge tool and the external proof are the SAME artifact. A ledger genuinely useful to you reads as more authentic to a hiring manager — so building for the mirror gives the window for free.

Connection 02 · the moat

Expose, don't hide

The moat is the willingness to show gaps, quiet months, fading repos and the agent mistakes you caught. Anyone can hide; almost no one volunteers their unflattering data. That volunteering is the proof.

Connection 05 · the atom

Open the evidence

The atomic unit of value: one-click drill-to-proof from any claim to inspectable source. Everything else is an index into this single, repeatable gesture — build this one feature.

Connection 06 · the meta-move

The self-auditing ledger

A ledger that maintains itself and adversarially audits itself — running the skeptic on its own behalf — is, itself, the ultimate work sample for an agentic-solutions role. The artifact is the demo.
